ROS 2 TSC applicant intake process [待校准@9364]

Objectives [待校准@9365]

  1. Increase the pool of high quality applicants [待校准@9366]

  2. Help applicants understand what the ROS 2 TSC expects from an application [待校准@9367]

  3. Help ROS 2 TSC better evaluate the applicants [待校准@9368]

Process [待校准@9369]

  1. Applicant inquires about joining TSC via info@openrobotics.org per ROS 2 Governance. [待校准@9370]

  2. Open Robotics responds with the application questions (below). [待校准@9371]

  3. Applicant completes and sends application to info@openrobotics.org. [待校准@9372]

  4. Open Robotics does an initial review and gives applicant feedback if warranted. For example: [待校准@9373]

    • "consider developing your track record of contribution and then re-apply." [待校准@9374]

    • "What you contribute to is not part of ROS 2 Common Packages (REP 2005). Consider getting it added before proceeding with application for more favorable reception." [待校准@9375]

  5. If application seems worthwhile, Open Robotics passes it to TSC member to "sponsor". [待校准@9376]

    • Open Robotics can ask TSC for volunteer(s) per applicant or Open Robotics could have list of TSC members who already volunteered to support this function. [待校准@9377]

  6. Sponsor arranges short 1:1 with applicant to talk about ROS 2 TSC role and responsibilities. Afterwards the applicant can: [待校准@9378]

    1. proceed with application as-is [待校准@9379]

    2. revise application [待校准@9380]

    3. delay submission e.g. to develop their contribution track record [待校准@9381]

    4. decide not to proceed. [待校准@9382]

  7. [two weeks before TSC meeting]: applications are distributed to TSC members for independent review [待校准@9383]

    • If 2 TSC members respond agreeing to "sponsor" applicant then these proceed to the next step. Applicant should "minimally meet" TSC member criteria before addressing TSC. [待校准@9384]

    • Otherwise Open Robotics informs applicant "Not at this time, please consider applying again later with more contribution history." [待校准@9385]

  8. [in TSC meeting] Applicant or Sponsor can address TSC. 5 minute presentation with up to 5 additional minutes of Q&A. [待校准@9386]

  9. [in TSC meeting]: Synchronous private/unsharable discussion of the applications after applicant(s) leave the call. TSC members are to be discrete about what has been discussed. [待校准@9387]

  10. [after TSC meeting]: Asynchronous vote on the applicants completed in around 10 days [待校准@9388]

  11. Open Robotics notifies applicant(s) of TSC decision. [待校准@9389]

Questionnaire for the applicant [待校准@9390]

The following is to be provided to the applicant by Open Robotics via email in response to them expressing interest in joining the ROS 2 TSC. [待校准@9391]

  1. To which aspect(s) of ROS 2 do you plan to make open source contributions? [待校准@9392]

    • For example contributions to ROS 2 Common Packages. [待校准@9393]

    • Are you already making those contributions? Since when? Please explain your history of contribution. If you have none, consider applying after developing some history of contribution. [待校准@9394]

    • Are there any other kinds of contributions that you are already making or plan? Education, evangelism, et al.? [待校准@9395]

  2. What level of effort do you plan to dedicate to making those contributions? [待校准@9396]

    • How many FTE (full time equivalent) people will be making those contributions? [待校准@9397]

    • How many FTE are already doing so? [待校准@9398]

  3. Are these contributions already happening now, and if so, can you point to results produced so far? [待校准@9399]

    • For example describe or attach details of your contributions to ROS 2 Common Packages. [待校准@9400]

    • Links to your contributions are helpful. [待校准@9401]

  4. How do these contributions benefit your business or organization? [待校准@9402]

    • Why do you do this? [待校准@9403]

Evaluation Guide for TSC members [待校准@9404]

The following is suggested guidance for TSC members to evaluate applicants: [待校准@9405]

  1. To which aspect(s) of ROS 2 do you plan to make open source contributions? [待校准@9392]

    • Are the contributions useful to the community? [待校准@9406]

    • Are the contributions to ROS 2 Common Packages? [待校准@9407]

  2. What level of effort do you plan to dedicate to making those contributions? [待校准@9396]

    • Give more credence to past contributions [待校准@9408]

    • Proposed future contributions are important but consider past contribution to evaluate how credible those plans are. [待校准@9409]

  3. Are these contributions already happening now, and if so, can you point to results produced so far? [待校准@9399]

    • Are the contributions ones that the community and TSC values? [待校准@9410]

    • Are the contributions to ROS 2 Common Packages (REP 2005)? [待校准@9411]

    • Emphasis is on what they’ve contributed already, not what they promise to do. If they don’t have enough evidence of contribution, encourage them to apply after developing their contribution track record. [待校准@9412]

  4. How do these contributions benefit your business or organization? [待校准@9402]

    • This answer helps you evaluate how much focus and investment it already has and whether it seems likely to continue. [待校准@9413]

    • Do you see that the applicant has a vested interest in this work and the success of ROS 2? Or does it seem they seek to join merely for prestige and marketing? [待校准@9414]
